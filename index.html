European Union vs. East European Countries

The above vizualization represents difference between 4 countries in europe (divided in two categories - European Union and Eastern Eaurope) depending how satisfied citizens are according to their employment status. Tiles that take larger space of the graph have better results.

The graph on the right represents citizen's satisfaction (X axis) on the scale from 1 indicating as most dissatisfied citizens to 10 - most satisfied ones and population in percentage (Y axis).

The red tiles that are hanging outside of the left graph are showing the highest value for the specific country. For informative purposes those values are highlihted in order to spot them easier.

How To Use the above Map ?

Hover with your mouse cursor over one of the colorfoul tiles. Each color represents different category (inner tiles) and country (outer tiles). Once you hover over a tile, the graph on the left will change according to the selection.

Conclusion

From this visualization it can be concluded that Sweden has population that is the most satisfied among other specified countries. As well, it cen be observed that Serbia is the country with the least population that are satisfied.
